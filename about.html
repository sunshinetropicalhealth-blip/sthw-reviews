Meet Marisol Caraballo, APRN

My name is Marisol Caraballo, APRN (NPI: 1851700553), and caring for others has been the purpose of my life for more than 30 years. Throughout my career, I’ve worked in adult and geriatric primary care, rheumatology, and spent 21 years in intensive care, where I learned the importance of compassion, patience, and truly listening to my patients.

I’ve dedicated my life to helping people manage chronic conditions, catching early warning signs, and guiding them toward healthier, longer, and more comfortable lives. As medicine has evolved, so has my passion for helping others feel their best inside and out. I became certified in aesthetics and wellness, completing advanced training in Botox, dermal fillers, IV nutrition therapy, medical weight-loss programs, and hormone replacement therapy. These services allow me to blend science with artistry, offering patients a chance to restore confidence and improve their overall well-being.

I care for every patient as if they were family, bringing decades of experience, genuine compassion, and a commitment to doing what’s best for them. My goal is simple: to use every bit of knowledge I’ve gained over the years to help you feel healthier, stronger, and truly cared for.
