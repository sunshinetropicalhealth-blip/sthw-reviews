Our Services

Breathe easy you’re in good hands. From telehealth to wellness care and home visiting, our team delivers friendly, family-focused support with the warm, reassuring care your health deserves.

Primary Care

Our primary care services include comprehensive exams and routine physicals designed to keep you healthy year-round. We focus on building long-term relationships with our patients, ensuring every visit supports your ongoing wellness and helps identify potential health issues early.

Chronic Condition Management

We provide personalized care plans for chronic health conditions such as Diabetes, Hypertension, Arthritis, Asthma, COPD, and Thyroid Disorders. Through regular monitoring, medication management, and lifestyle guidance, we help patients maintain stability, reduce symptoms, and improve daily quality of life.

Routine & Sick (At-Home Visit)

Along with Preventive care options we provide at-home visits with convenient medical care in the comfort of your home. These visits focus on reviewing health screenings, evaluating current symptoms, checking vital signs, and providing personalized guidance for maintaining or improving your health. This service supports both routine wellness needs and minor medical concerns while helping patients stay proactive about their overall care. Only accepting in or around Polk County, Florida.

Telehealth

Weight Loss Program

Our medical weight loss program is designed to help you achieve healthy, sustainable results. We offer personalized plans that may include nutritional counseling, lifestyle coaching, lab work, and medication options when appropriate. Each plan is tailored to your goals, metabolism, and overall health to support long-term success.

Wellness Injections & Shot Treatments

We offer professional shot treatments and therapeutic vitamin injections designed to support overall wellness and energy levels. Services include Vitamin B12, Energy Booster, Vitamin D support, NAD+, Odansetron, Lipostat, Lipominus injections; folic acid supplementation, and Codensantron-based injections as clinically appropriate.
