Patient Rights Sunshine Tropical Health & Wellness

We are committed to respectful, safe, and transparent care. This page summarizes common patient rights and expectations when receiving services through Sunshine Tropical Health & Wellness, LLC.

Effective
January 2026
Applies To
In-Person + Telehealth

Respect and Non-Discrimination

You have the right to be treated with dignity and respect. We do not tolerate discrimination or harassment.

Clear Communication

You have the right to understandable information about your care, recommended services, and next steps. You may ask questions and request clarification at any time.

Privacy and Confidentiality

You have the right to privacy. We handle your information in accordance with applicable privacy requirements and our website privacy policy.

Participation in Care Decisions

You have the right to participate in decisions about your care, including the right to accept or refuse recommended services, when clinically appropriate.

Safe Care Environment

You have the right to services delivered with reasonable safety practices and professional standards.

Billing Transparency

You have the right to ask questions about fees and billing. We aim to communicate pricing and expectations clearly whenever possible.

Concerns and Complaints

If you have a concern about your experience, we want to hear it. You may contact us using the information below, and we will make a good-faith effort to address concerns promptly and professionally.
